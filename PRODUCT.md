# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Internal telesales agents, supervisors, and administrators.

## Product Purpose
An internal telesales and administrative system that enables agents to efficiently manage outbound/inbound calls, view lead details, and track call histories, while allowing supervisors and administrators to manage operations, filters, and workflows. Success is measured by agent efficiency, call throughput, and data clarity.

## Brand Personality
- **Efficient**: Speed and keyboard-friendliness are front and center.
- **Professional**: Clean, enterprise-grade interface that prioritizes data density and utility over decoration.
- **Clear**: High-contrast, structured layout with zero visual clutter.

## Anti-references
- Avoid generic SaaS gradients and decorative color splashes.
- Avoid excessive animation and heavy motion curves.
- Avoid glassmorphism, soft wide shadows, and "ghost-card" borders.
- Avoid nested cards and complex layout hierarchies where flat lists/grids are more appropriate.

## Design Principles
- **Data First**: Prioritize readability of dense data, clear tabular structures, and robust filtering options.
- **Zero Friction**: Keep interactions direct and optimized for repeated daily workflows.
- **Clear Hierarchy**: Use size, weight, and subtle colors (e.g., status badges) to indicate priority, not decorative assets.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **High Contrast**: Ensure body text and status indicators exceed WCAG AA requirements (>= 4.5:1 ratio).
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Ensure inputs, status changes, and dialing controls are accessible and efficient without relying solely on mouse clicks.
